The Writers Coalition

The writers coaltion is a group of writers that are dedicated to sharing their ideas and creativity with the comunity. There are 4 members so far.

Rules of the Coalition

There aren't that many rules, but the ones there are are followed without complaint. Let's look at all the listed ones.

We also have a special writers coalition chat. The first seven rules are specifically for the chat.

  1. No long words
  2. No editing someone else's message
  3. No inviting other people without the owner’s permission
  4. Always add timestamps
  5. No changing the font, size, or bold for the words.
  6. No swearing
  7. Try to talk about writing/story idea related stuff
  8. Golden rule: don’t spill the beans about someone else’s story
